### 1.2 Training Limitini Haftalik Yap
Dosya: backend/src/routes/slug.ts — /train endpoint

Mevcut: Gunluk 2 training limiti
Yeni: Haftalik limit (free slug 1/hafta, snail 2/hafta)

Training limit sorgusunu degistir:
Eski: WHERE snail_id = $1 AND DATE(started_at) = CURRENT_DATE
Yeni: WHERE snail_id = $1 AND started_at >= date_trunc('week', CURRENT_TIMESTAMP)

### 1.3 Mini-Game Limitini Wallet Bazli Yap
Dosya: backend/src/routes/slug.ts — /mini-game endpoint

Mevcut: Snail bazli gunluk limit
Yeni: Wallet bazli gunluk 5 limit (herkes icin ayni)

Limit sorgusunu degistir:
SELECT COALESCE(SUM(dmp.count), 0) as total_count
FROM daily_minigame_plays dmp
JOIN slugs s ON dmp.snail_id = s.id
WHERE s.wallet = $1 AND dmp.play_date = $2

dailyLimit = 5 (free slug farki kaldirilsin)

### 1.4 Tactic Action Snail Sahipligi Dogrula
Dosya: backend/src/routes/race.ts — /action endpoint

Action submit edildiginde snail'in wallet'a ait oldugunu dogrula:
const owner = await getOne("SELECT id FROM slugs WHERE id = $1 AND wallet = $2", [snailId, wallet]);
if (owner === null) { res.status(403).json({ error: "Not your snail" }); return; }

### 1.5 GP Tier Gate Ekle
Dosya: backend/src/routes/race.ts — /join endpoint

GP katiliminda tier kontrolu:
- GP ve free_slug ise: red ("GP requires at least a Snail")
- GP ve entry_fee > 150 ve tier < 2 ise: red ("Gold GP requires Elite snail (Tier 2+)")

Frontend (RaceLobby.tsx): GP secildiginde snail listesini filtrele — free slug'lari cikar, Gold GP'de tier<2 cikar.

### 1.6 Stat Cap Guvenlik Kontrolu
Dosya: backend/src/routes/slug.ts

Training claim ve mini-game stat gain hesaplarinda:
Mevcut: Math.min(gain, cap - current)
Yeni: Math.min(gain, Math.max(0, cap - (current || 0)))

Bu degisikligi /claim-training ve /mini-game endpoint'lerine uygula.

### 1.7 Weather Quest Tetikleme Duzelt

Mevcut: if (count <= 3) ...
